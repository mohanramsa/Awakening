Level I · The Monkey Mind

The Monkey
Mind

Restless Thoughts

🎨 Visual Scene

A human silhouette sits cross-legged in a dark blue cosmic space. Glowing thought-bubbles orbit the head like chaotic clouds. Stars fill the background. A warm golden aura pulses around the meditator.

🧘 Focus Mind

Thoughts appear as floating clouds around the player. Hold spacebar slowly to synchronize breathing. As the rhythm stabilizes, clouds dissolve one by one.

Goal: Calm the mind through breath.

River of
Emotions

Anger · Fear · Desire

🎨 Visual Scene

A glowing river flows through a dark spiritual landscape. Waves pulse with colors of anger (crimson), fear (violet) and desire (amber). A meditating figure sits at the shore — watching, never reacting.

Level III · Self Inquiry
🔍

The
Question

Mirror of Light · The Seer

🎨 Visual Scene

A mystical temple with glowing question symbols floating in golden space. The character stands before a mirror of pure light — inside, the reflection dissolves into energy particles.

🧘 Focus Mind

Floating questions appear one by one:

  • Who am I?
  • Am I the body?
  • Am I the mind?
  • Who is aware of thoughts?

Solve reflection puzzles. Correct answers unlock deeper awareness.

Goal: Discover the seer behind the seeker.

Lesson
"The seeker begins to discover the seer."

A vast cosmic starfield of deep purple and gold. A luminous human silhouette stands at the center. Thoughts drift past as glowing stars — the figure remains perfectly still, attached to nothing.
